Loggin' Day Tea:
The beginning of something bitter-sweet (or silly).

For this project I wanted to make a more playful illustration to go on a more serious product --like an imaginary alcoholic tea brand. This has been a modern design choice used by a few companies in recent years and I wanted to try my hand at it.

I started with a few sketches and made my way to Procreate where I cleaned it up. From there I moved to illustrator and made the entire illustration vectorized/scalable. The flaux hand-drawn style utilizes Illustrator brushes, and really ended up tying the whole piece together.
