Local Bean:
A coffee compendium in your pocket.

Local Bean is an app for absolute coffee snobs. I designed the original brand identity to represent a local, yet touristy experience when checking out cafes all over America. The icon went through several revisions, where I meant to simplify the forms so that It would work at large and small scales.

App & Web Design

At the beginning of this project I wanted to build the app as user-friendly as possible. Sometimes people dont think about how much time it actually takes to design an entire app, but most of the time goes into the user experience rather than the aesthetics of it.

The app has a few sections: The map (which I knew would integrate with Google Map's API), the social page, the profile page, and lastly the store page(which would come later in development).
