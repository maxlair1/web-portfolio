Joy Visual Identity for the Bellbrook Bible Church mens weekend.

Visual Identity, Illustration, Adobe Illustrator CC 2022


© 2022 Max Lair

Design built to encapsulate the joyful feeling expressed in Philippians and Nehemiah. The harvest is plentiful, but the laborers are few.

I designed this piece for the annual BBC Men’s Retreat. The etching style provided both high visual interest and tireless effort. The design means to take the oxymoron of “joy” and “work” and apply it to the Christian life. Often a Christian has joy in seemingly joy-less circumstances, and Philippians 1:21 exists to reinstate the joy even in work. At the same time, “the harvest is plentiful, but the labourers are few.” Each one of the hatch-marks that give the illusion of shading were hand drawn and positioned in Adobe Illustrator. The entire work is scalable.
