About Me

I was born and raised in Springboro, OH. I began my work life as a janitor, bringing my high attention to detail into the corporate world. Like many others, I moved up by seeking to merge my passions and hobbies with my work-life. This provided some amazing opportunities in the field of design. I short after began my graphic design education at Cedarville University.

Today, I have transferred to Liberty University, where I am pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design, while working full time and studying front-end development/web design on the side.

Currently, I am living in Dayton, OH, working at LifeQuest Studios as a full-time Graphic Designer, whilst also taking freelance projects on the side. I continue to live my life with anticipation and excitement about what is next to come, in the field and also in my current work environment
